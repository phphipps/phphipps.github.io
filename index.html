About Me

I am a PhD student in the Astronomy Department at Boston University. My research focus is the atmospheres of the gas giant planets in our solar system and how they interact with their magnetic fields. I am also interested in the interactions of the satellites of those planets with the planetary magnetic field and their effects on the system.
