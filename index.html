About Me

I received my PhD in Astronomy in May 2019. I am currently an Assistant Research Scientist at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County based at NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center with the Center for Research and Exploration in Space Science and Technology. My research is on the lunar topography and how it shields the surface from space radiation. I am also interested in the magnetic fields of the outer planets in our solar system (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune) and how the satellites of those planets interact with the planetary magnetic field on the system.
